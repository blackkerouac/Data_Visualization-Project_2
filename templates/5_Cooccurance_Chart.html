COOCURRANCE CHART

  • Co-occurance Chart Example

    • The theory behind the co-occurence chart is to show how frequently certain relationships occur together
    • For example, in this Les Mis co-occurence we see that characters that appear in the same scenes have a stronger co-occurence relationship
    • For my co-occurence chart I wanted to show how often certain identities intersected in survey respondents
    • We found in our data that there was one respondent who was, vegan, Latino, and Christian
    • We also found 10 male vegetarians with graduate degrees
    • Had the occurence chart gone according to plan, it would have showed those relationships between identities
    • These are the demographic categories the cooccurance chart would have shown:
      • Gender
      • Education
      • Political Affiliation
      • Race/Ethnicity
      • Religion
