Definitions

  • Omnivore = A person who eats food of both plant and animal orgin.

  • Vegetarian = a person who excludes meat from their diet but may eat other animal products such as eggs, dairy and honey.

  • Vegan = a person who does not eat any products derived from animals.

*Caveat: There are degrees of all eater types. For example, there are omnivores who don't eat red meat, vegetarians who don't eat eggs and vegans who do eat honey, etc.
